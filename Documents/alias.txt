I’ve decided on the name.

They will know me only through my work - not my birth name, not my face.

“Yogeshwari” - Not for worship. Not for war. But because even the divine can walk in shadows.

She was the goddess of control, of inner strength, of unseen power. This project needs to be all of those things. What I’m building must be untraceable. Like a breath in deep water.

From now on, this research - this resistance - lives under that name.
