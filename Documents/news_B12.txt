NAVY COMMANDER CAPTURED
-----------------------

Commander Ajith Boyagoda was taken by LTTE Sea Tigers in an early morning raid. Defence officials have not disclosed full details.

In an unrelated report, fishermen have found rusted mechanical parts along the Jaffna coastline. Navy spokesperson dismissed them as remnants of illegal trawler operations.

Some villagers noted military-style rubber seals and coded stamping - unconfirmed by authorities.
