Comparison Summary:

MORSE VS SPECTROGRAPH

MORSE:
+ Universal, can be tapped or tonal
+ Easily decoded with manual skill
- Obvious if listener is trained

SPECTROGRAPH:
+ Hidden in frequency domain
+ Cannot be detected casually
- Requires playback speed modulation
- Not portable (requires visual tools)
